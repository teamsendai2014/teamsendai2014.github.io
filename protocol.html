Whole device

First experiment

1.All enzymes and DNA molecules were mixed after annaling. Input-AB or BA/Template complex, Transducer, and Gate were formed by annealing(cooling from 95 to 20℃ for 75min, -1℃/min).
2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted for various time (1,2,5,10,12,,15,20,25,30,60,100 min), then these were left at 80℃ for 30 minutes. After that, all samples were left at room temperature by the time of applying (more than 1 hour).
3.15% acrylamide gel was formed with 1×TBE buffer.
4.The electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 86 min.
5.Imaging by gel imager (BioRad) with Green light.

Second experiment

1.Input-AB or BA/Template complex, Transducer, and Gate were formed by annealing(cooling from 95 to 20℃ for 75min, -1℃/min). All samples were mixed after the formation of these complexes.
2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted for various time (1/6,1/2.1,2,5,10,,12,15,20,25,30,40 min), then these were left at 80℃ for 30 minutes. After that, all samples were annealed again for 35 minutes(cooling from 95 to 70℃ for 25min, -1℃/min, then cooling from 70 to 20℃ for 10min, -5℃/min).
3.15% acrylamide gel was formed with 1×TBE buffer.
4.The electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 85 min.
5.Imaging by gel imager with Green light.

Updating Process

1.Input-Template complex and Gate were formed by annealing (95 to 20℃, -1℃/min).
2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted at 45℃ for 3 hour in thermal cycler.
3.Gel-electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 75 minutes with 15% acrylamide gel. Then gel was stained by SYBR Gold.Then gel was stained by SYBR Gold.
4.Imaging by gel imager (BioRad).

Go back to experiment

Combination of 3 processes

1.Input/Template complex and Output complex were formed by annealing(cooling from 95 to 20℃ for 75min, -1℃/min). All samples were mixed after the formation of these complexes.
2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted for various time (1,2,5,10,20,50 min), then these were left at 80℃ for 20 min. After that, all samples were left at room temperature by the time of applying (more than 1 hour).
3.15% acrylamide gel was formed with 1×TBE buffer.
4.The electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 75 min.
5.Imaging by gel imager with Blue LED (Non-stained gel image).
6.Gel was stained by SYBR Gold then imaged with UV light (Stained gel image).

Component (Seesaw gate)

Measurement of fluorescence

1.Input was annealed at the consentration of 10μM. The annealing was conducted for 75 minutes (from 95 to 20℃: 1℃ per 1 minutes).
2.Fluorescence intensity was measured by spectrofluorometer(FP6200, JASCO) for 5000 seconds per 10 seconds.
The wavelength of excitation light was 492 nm. The fluorescence intensity of 515 nm light was measured.
Transducer, Gate, and Trigger were mixtured before the addition of Input.
