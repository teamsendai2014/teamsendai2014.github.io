Function confirmation of 3 processes

Reading Process

1.Input and Template was annaled to form the complex.
Annealing condition: 95 to 20℃ (-1℃/min)

2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted at 37℃/55℃ for 1 hour in thermal cycler.

3.Formation of 20% acrylamide gel

table. Formation of 20% acrylamide gel
30% acrylamide 6.66 ml
10×TBE 1 ml
mQ 2.33 ml
10%APS 100 μl
TEMED 10 μl
Total 10 ml

4.Gel-electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 50 minutes. Then gel was stained by SYBR Gold.

5.Imaging by gel imager（BioRad)

Releasing Process

1.Anchor, Output, and Key were mixed and then annealed from 95 to 20℃ (-1℃/min).

2.Enzymatic reaction was conducted at 37℃/55℃ for 1 hour in thermal cycler.



3.Gel-electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 100 minutes with 20% acrylamide gel. Then gel was stained by SYBR Gold.Then gel was stained by SYBR Gold.

4.Imaging by gel imager（BioRad)

Updating Process

1.Input-Template complex and Gate were formed by annealing (95 to 20℃, -1℃/min).

シーソーゲートの機能確認 by spectrometer

1.The mixture of I and I* strand were annealed at the consentration of 10μM. The annealing was conducted for 75 minutes (from 95 to 20℃: 1℃ per 1 minutes). We named this sample I-I* complex.
2.Fluorescence intensity was measured by spectrofluorometer(FP6200, 日本分光株式会社) for 5000 seconds per 10 seconds.
The wavelength of excitation light was 492 nm. The fluorescence intensity of 515 nm light was measured.
mO*, Input complex, and Trigger were added in the 10 seconds time interval of measurement.

シーソーゲートの機能確認 by electrophoresis

1.Input complex and Output complex were formed by annealing(cooling from 95 to 20℃ for 75min, -1℃/min). All samples were mixed after the formation of these complexes.
2.15% acryl amide gel was formed with 1×TAE buffer.
3.The electrophoresis was conducted at 100V for 75 min.
4.Imaging by gel imager with Blue LED (Non-stained gel image).
5.Gel was stained by SYBR Gold then imaged with UV light (Stained gel image).
