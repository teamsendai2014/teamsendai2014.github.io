Design

Enzyme device

Here, we explain the device using DNA and enzymes.

First, we explain rough mechanisms of the device.

The device is composed of input DNA and three processes. Input DNA codes sequence of Output. Three processes are reading process, releasing process and updating process.
In reading process, Input DNA gives information to the device. With the first instruction, a signal corresponding to information is released and starts cascades of next processes.
In releasing process, Output is released according to the signal from the previous process.
After releasing process finished, updating process starts.
In updating process, the first instruction is removed from Input DNA for the next cycle.
By cycling these three processes step by step, single stranded DNAs are generated in order.
Secondly, we explain the detail of three mechanisms.

The device is composed of Templates, Transducer and Gates. (fig.O)

Reading process utilize DNA amplification scheme based on the repeated extension and nicking of one strand of DNA duplex. This process is composed of input DNA, single stranded DNA(templates), polymerase and nickase.
Reading process is achieved by following chemical reactions.

Step1. Templates hybridize with input DNA.
Step2. Polymerase elongates DNA from 3' end. To limit point elongated by polymerase, Input/Templates complex contains some toholds.
Step3. Nickase cleaves upper side of a DNA duplex.
Step4. Polymerase elongates DNA from 3' end again with strand displacement activity . As a result, single stranded DNA is released.

Repeating Step3 and Step4, single stranded DNA which is named Signal is amplified. Signal starts next processes: releasing process and updating process.

Releasing process is based on polymerase reaction and strand displacement. In this process, the device releases single stranded DNA.
This process is composed of Signal, double stranded DNA (Transducer ), and polymerase.
Releasing process is implemented by following chemical reactions.

Step1. Signal hybridizes with a part of Transducer.
Step2. Polymerase elongates DNA from 3' end.
Step3. Output DNA are released by the strand displacement activity.

These reactions take place just after reading process because the toehold region of Transducer is slightly longer than that of gates.


Updating process is achieved by strand displacement and restriction enzyme. This process is composed of Signal, double stranded DNA (gate) and restriction enzyme.
Updating process is implemented by following chemical reactions.

Step1. Signal hybridizes with a part of Gate.
Step2. Polymerase elongates DNA from 3' end.
Step3. Single stranded DNA (Updater) is released by strand displacement activity.
Step4. Updater hybridizes with Input/template complex.
Step5. Restriction enzyme cleaves duplex of Updater and Input/template complex. Input is then updated.

Input is updated for the next cycle, the three processes can take place from the beginning.


The cycle is repeated again and again until all the instructions coded (written) in the Input are executed. Eventually, the Outputs are released in order. Programmability is an important feature of our device, because different Input can result in different order to release Outputs.

Enzyme free device

Here,we explain the mechanisms of enzyme free device(using only DNA). Enzyme free device is designed based on seesaw gates capable of cascading strand displacement reactions.

Components of the device are Input , Trigger, Gate, Transducer and Fuel.
The order to release outputs is only coded in the input, while other DNA components is utilized for cascading the reactions.



Cascading reactions are implemented by following steps.

Step1. Trigger hybridizes with Input. A signal is then released by strand displacement reaction.

Step2. The signal released in Step1 hybridizes with Transducer. A first output is released by strand displacement.
This reaction takes place faster than other reactions so that Transducer serves as a function of
threshold.

Step3. After Step2 finished, remaining Signal starts to hybridize with Gate. As a result, a new trigger is then
released.Signal is used as a catalyst in this step by the function of Fuel which hybridizes with
 Signal/Gate complex and restores Signal. By consuming the excess Fuell , the reaction progresses
from a small amount of Signal.

Step4. The new trigger hybridizes with input. A new Signal is then released which starts next cascades

The device repeats the cascading reaction until all Outputs are released. Eventually Outputs are released in order. As well as Enzyme device, programmability is an important feature.
