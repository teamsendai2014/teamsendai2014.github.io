Discussion

Enzyme device

In this section, we contrast the result of simulation and experiments and discuss differences between these two results. As shown in previous section, the reaction velocity in simulation is more 100 times greater than in experiments. Perhaps the cause of this difference is in reaction rate constant or reaction process in simulation. Rate coefficient of reaction in simulation is quoted from references. However, we equally decided the reaction velocity in all reaction except gate and the reaction velocity of hybridization with gate DNA is one-tenth as low as that of the value. By right, the reaction velocity of hybridization with gate DNA is a lower. Therefore if we determine these reaction rate constants by using experiments and apply the values to simulation, we will perform accurate simulation. In each process, some single strand DNAs are released and in simulation, they are released endlessly. However a quantity of dNTP is limited. Thus they converge on a constant value. Therefore if we apply some treatment to make Signal be a constant value, we can perform accurate simulation. Moreover in simulation DNA denature- reaction does not occur, but actually there are not a few denature reaction. We should experiment about this reaction and consider the impact in simulation. 簡易ではあるが、我々はシミュレーションと実験で得られたデータのフィッティングを試みた。前述した問題点を考慮して、ゲートとシグナルのハイブリダイゼーションの反応速度定数を5.0*10^5から5.0*10^2に、その他で起こるハイブリダイゼーションの反応速度定数を5.0*10^6から5.0*10^4に変えた。またシグナルが際限なく出続けているのに関しては、シグナルのODEに減少項を加え、一定値に収束するようにした。 　下の図がフィッティングを行って得られたグラフである。

Enzyme-free device

The simulated curve of Output concentration well discribes the experimental results except for the initial transient. This difference is mainly beause the thresholding mechanisum did not work perfectly.


Fig.2 Result of simulation without the function of threshold
The thresholding is caused by the difference of reaction rate constant of two hybridizations: Signal to Output and Signal to Gate.
We set same number for that constant in this simulation.

In our design, Signal must hybridize to Transducer before it binds to Gate for thresholding. However, it is difficult to remove all the Signal molecule at the beginning of the reaction because of inperfect purification.
